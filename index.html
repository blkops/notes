    Notes

    This repository is meant to hold notes from meetings so people can catch up on where we left off. Feel free to add by adhering to the format:

    YYYY-MM-DD-HH-MM-MEETING_NAME-USERNAME.markdown

    Note that HH and MM should be when the meeting started, not necessarily when you started recording. Also, replace the spaces in the meeting name with underscores.
